## What is Linqra?

Linqra is an AI App Store built on our production-proven API gateway architecture. While offering a marketplace for AI applications, Linqra maintains its core strength as a robust gateway, providing enterprise-grade security, routing, and resilience for both traditional and AI services. This dual capability ensures organizations can confidently expand their AI capabilities while maintaining strict control over their entire API infrastructure.
